Biography

Shu Wei is a Visiting Professor at the School of Statistics, University of Costa Rica and also a associated researcher at the Centro de Investigación en Matemática Pura y Aplicada (CIMPA). His research interests include statistical methods, time series, spatiotemporal analysis and their applications.
